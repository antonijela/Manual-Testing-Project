## ID: 48

## Test Case Name: Verify that the admin can edit tracker logs.

## Pre-Conditions:
- The admin must be logged in (refer to Test Case ID 1 for valid login credentials).
- At least one performance tracker and its associated log must exist in the system.

## Steps:
1. Navigate to the left sidebar and click on "Performance."
   - Expected result: The "Manage Reviews" page is displayed.

2. Click on "My Trackers" at the top.
   - Expected result: The "My Trackers" page is displayed.

3. In the "Record Found" list, locate the desired performance tracker and click "View" under the "Actions" column.
   - Expected result: The detailed page for the selected tracker is displayed.

4. For the desired log, click on the three vertical dots at the right end of the log and select the "Edit" option.
   - Expected result: The "Edit Tracker Log" form appears.

5. Edit the fields: update the "Log" field, select either "Positive" or "Negative," and modify the "Comment" field.
   - Expected result: The updated log details are visible.

6. Click the "Save" button.
   - Expected result: A success notification appears, and the modified tracker log is updated with a pencil icon and the date when it was last modified.
