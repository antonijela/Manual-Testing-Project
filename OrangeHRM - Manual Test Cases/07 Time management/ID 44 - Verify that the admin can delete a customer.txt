## ID: 44

## Test Case Name: Verify that the admin can delete a customer.

## Pre-Conditions:
- The admin must be logged in (refer to Test Case ID 1 for valid login credentials).

## Steps:
1. Navigate to the left sidebar and click on "Time."
   - Expected result: The Timesheets page is displayed.

2. Click on the "Project Info" option at the top.
   - Expected result: The Project Info dropdown is displayed.

3. Select "Customers" from the dropdown.
   - Expected result: The Customers page is displayed.

4. From the list, next to the desired customer, click the trash icon under the "Actions" column.
   - Expected result: A prompt is displayed asking for confirmation to delete the customer.

5. Click "Yes, Delete."
   - Expected result: A confirmation message is displayed, and the customer is successfully deleted from the list.
